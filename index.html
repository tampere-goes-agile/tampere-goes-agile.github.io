THEME 2014 is *We're all in this together*. With this theme, we want to focus on collaboration and variety of skills we need in successful software development.

Our call for proposals is closed and selections have been made. The site will update with the talk contents by 14.9.

This year's event will fit 140 participants. The event if free of charge and the enrollment will be done in two phases. The first phase (getting listed) enrollment opens 15.9. 12.00 and you can secure your place being on time with the enrollment, as we work with first come first served principle. Second phase (confirming for food) enrollment will be targeted for those who got listed in the first phase and you will need to confirm for entrance. The two phases are there to emphasize that we find the community - people's presence - in a conference like this of utmost value, and we would like people who enroll and confirm to actually be there. The discussions you can give to the others by being there is the reason we organize these! So welcome!

This year's program includes a change of location to Teerenpeli at the end of the talks. Please try to schedule so that you could be there to enjoy the atmosphere and program that continues.
